汤智聪

你好，我是汤智聪。我今年十六岁，属龙。我有黑头发和黑眼睛。我觉得我很高，可是我的朋友也很高。我的朋友常常喜欢跟我去打球和跑步。他们最喜欢打篮球，但是我最喜欢跑步。我们周末喜欢看电影。我们都喜欢吃米饭和喝水。我有一个朋友叫李定右，是韩国人。他也有黑头发和黑眼睛。他最喜欢看的电影是 “Frozen”，我也喜欢看 “Frozen”。我的家有四口人，妈妈，爸爸，妹妹，和我。我的妹妹十四岁，八年级。她喜欢喝汽水和吃冰淇淋。我的爸爸是大学老师。每天他五点半回家。我的妈妈在Columbia大学工作。她每天六点半回家。等我的妹妹和我上大学的时候，他们就会退休。

Hello, my name is Tang Zhi Cong. I am 16, born in the year of the dragon. I have black hair and black eyes. I think I'm pretty tall, however my friends are also very tall. My friends often enjoy playing ball and running with me. They like to play basketball the most, but I like running the most. On the weekends, we like to go to the movies. We like to eat rice and drink water. I have a friend named Li Ding You. He is Korean. He also has black hair and black eyes. His favorite movie is "Frozen." My favorite movie is also "Frozen." I have four people in my family; mom, dad, little sister, and I. My sister is 14 and in eighth grade. She likes to drink soda and eat ice cream. My dad is a college teacher. Every day he comes home at 5:30 PM. My mom works at Columbia University. Every day she comes home at 6:30 PM. After my sister and I go to college, they will retire.

学校

I go to River Dell High School, and I am in 11th grade. Every day I go to school at 7:30 AM. My mom drives me to school. Other than Chemistry major, I'm also taking Mandarin III. My Chinese teacher's name is Teacher Ho. She is a very good teacher. She teaches us grammar and how to write Chinese characters. Every week she gives us a new lesson. Every day we get out of school at 2:30, however I don't get home until 6:30 PM. After school, I run. I run very fast. When I get home I do my homework. Every day I don't have too much homework.

我上 River Dell 高中，十一年级。每天我七点半上学。我妈妈开车送我上学。除了化学专业, 我也上中文三。我的中文老师叫Ho老师。她是一个很好的老师。她教我们语法和写中文字。每个星期她给我们新课文。每天我们两点半放学，但是我每天六点半才回家。放学的时候我去跑步，跑得快。我回家的时候就做功课。每天我没有太多功课。

日记

三月十一号

今天我的朋友给我打了电话。他请我去打球，可是我给他说我明天有中文考试。明天我的中文老师会考我闷得语法。她说如果我们都考好了，她就会带我们去动物园里玩。我的老师最喜欢去动物园看熊猫。今天放学的时候，我跟我的朋友去了图书馆复习中文。回家的时候我还练习写汉字。现在我很累了。我想睡觉了，但是我还有化学功课。我把我的功课都做完了，我就可以睡觉。

March 11th

Today my friend called me and asked me to play ball, but I told him I have a Chinese test tomorrow. Tomorrow my teacher will test us on grammar. She said if we all do well, she will take us to the zoo. She loves to go to the zoo to look at the pandas. Today after school, I went to the library with my friends to review Chinese. When I got home, I continued to practice writing Chinese characters. I am very tired. I want to sleep, however I still have chemistry homework. After I finish all my homework, I will be able to sleep.

一封信

Li Ding You,

This semester I am taking Mandarin III. I don’t think Chinese is very hard, but my classmates think it is. Because I'm Chinese, I normally don't have to study much. If we have a test, I usually help my friend review. His name is Li You. He is not Chinese. He is Korean, so he thinks Chinese class is very hard. Yesterday our teacher taught us Grammar. She also taught us how to write characters. Because we have a big test tomorrow, after I eat dinner today I will go to Li You's house to help him practice Chinese. It's not very convenient to go to his house, but he is my good friend.

Every day we get out of school at 2:30 PM. Right after school I go to run. I think I run pretty fast, but my friends are faster. I went home at 6:30 PM. I normally don't have a lot of homework, so I usually watch TV while doing it. Sometimes Li You gives me a call and asks me to play basketball. I suck at basketball, so I don’t like to go with him. I enjoy using the computer to go on the internet very much. Li You doesn’t know how to use the computer, so I usually teach him how to get on the internet.

How are you? Are you taking Chinese class as well? Next month our school will have concert. Do you think you can come? I think you're pretty cool!



Wish you well,
Tang Zhi Cong

李定右,

这个学期我上中文三。我不觉得中文课难，但是我的同学觉得中文太难了。因为我是中国人，我平常不需要学习。如果我们有考试，我常常帮我的朋友复习。他的名字是李有。他不是中国人。他是韩国人，所以他觉得中文课很难。昨天老师教我们新的语法。她也教我们写汉字。因为明天我们有一个很大的考试，所以我今天晚上吃完晚以后，我一定要去李有的家帮他练习中文。去他家很不方便，但是他是我的好朋友。

我每天两点半放学。下课的时候我就去跑步。我觉得我跑得很快，可是我的同学比我快。我六点半就回家。我平常没有很多功课，所以我常常一边看电视一边做功课。有的时候李有给我打电话，请我去打篮球。我打篮球打得不好，所以我不喜欢跟他去。我很喜欢用电脑上网。李有不知道怎么用电脑，所以我常常教他上网。

你怎么样？你也上中文课吗？下个月我的学校有一个音乐会，你可以来吗？我觉得你很酷！ 



祝好，
汤智聪
